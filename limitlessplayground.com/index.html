About the Limitless Playground

The Gorham Parks and Recreaction Department along with Northern Human Services, Community Services Center, the Family Support Council and a group of energetic parents are working together to build a new ADA accessable playground on the Gorham Common. And we need your help! We are hoping to raise enough funds to break ground and install a few pieces of equipment this coming spring 2020. We will be working on fundraisers and grant opportunities in the near future as the entire project will cost around $70,000.00. Through word of mouth of this project, we have already raised $14,562! This project will be done in phases over a number of years as the cost is high, but with your help, and the help of others, we can reach our goal much sooner. We are planning on having a wheelchair swing, a wheelchair whirl, a glide along and several interactive features. This equipment is not only handicap accessible, but children of all abilities can enjoy playing together in our new inclusive playground.
